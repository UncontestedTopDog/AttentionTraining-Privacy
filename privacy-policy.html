Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Attention Training ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how WillsIdea collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Attention Training mobile application (the "App").

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you understand how your data is handled. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully before using the App.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

2.1 Training Data (Stored Locally)

  • Your training records and game progress
  • Training scores and performance statistics
  • Training session history and preferences

Important: All your training data is stored locally on your device and is NOT uploaded to our servers. We do not have access to your personal training records.

2.2 Analytics Data

We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymized usage statistics, including:

  • App usage patterns and feature interactions
  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version and performance metrics
  • General geographic region (country-level only)

2.3 Crash Data

We collect crash logs and diagnostic data through Firebase Crashlytics to help us identify and fix bugs. This data includes:

  • Device model and operating system
  • App state at the time of crash
  • Stack traces and error logs

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Improve the App's functionality and user experience
  • Identify and fix technical issues and bugs
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance training features
  • Ensure app stability and performance

4. Third-Party Services

We use Firebase, a service provided by Google, for analytics and crash reporting. Firebase may collect certain information as described in Google's Privacy Policy. You can learn more about Firebase's data practices at:

https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

5. Data Retention

  • Local Data: Your training data remains on your device until you delete the App or clear the app data.
  • Analytics Data: Firebase Analytics data is retained according to Google's data retention policies, typically for 14 months.
  • Crash Data: Crash logs are retained for 90 days.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Deletion: Delete your local training data by uninstalling the App
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics through your device settings

For European Users (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights including the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, data portability, and object to processing of your personal data.

For California Users (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information is collected, request deletion of your data, and opt-out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell your personal information.

7. Children's Privacy

Attention Training is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information. Your training data is stored locally on your device and is protected by your device's security features. Analytics data transmitted to Firebase is encrypted in transit.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and updating the "Effective Date" above. Your continued use of the App after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
